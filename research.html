Core Architecture Design

Research Framework
Neural-Symbolic Integration • BERT • Forward-Chaining • Modular Design 2024

NeuroLogicX represents a novel approach to artificial intelligence that seamlessly integrates neural networks with symbolic reasoning systems through a carefully designed modular architecture, achieving state-of-the-art performance while maintaining explainability crucial for trustworthy AI systems.

Architecture Pipeline:

  • Neural Perception: BERT-based entity extraction and semantic understanding
  • Symbolic Translation: Predicate generation from neural outputs
  • Forward Chaining: Logical inference with rule application
  • Explainable Output: Complete reasoning traces with confidence scoring
  • Confidence Calibration: Reliable uncertainty quantification for deployment

System Integration:

  • Modular design enabling component-level testing and improvement
  • Seamless data flow between neural and symbolic components
  • Real-time processing capabilities for interactive applications
  • Extensible architecture supporting additional reasoning modules
  • Comprehensive logging for research reproducibility
