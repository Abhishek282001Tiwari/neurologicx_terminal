# Research Framework

# NeuroLogicX Architecture & Methodology

Technical details of the novel neurosymbolic framework that seamlessly integrates neural networks with symbolic reasoning systems, achieving state-of-the-art performance while maintaining full explainability.

## Technical Implementation

<div class="project featured">
    <div class="project-header">
        <h3>Core Architecture Design</h3>
        <span class="project-status">Research Framework</span>
    </div>
    <div class="project-meta">
        <span class="project-tech">Neural-Symbolic Integration • BERT • Forward-Chaining • Modular Design</span>
        <span class="project-date">2024</span>
    </div>
    <p class="project-description">
        NeuroLogicX represents a novel approach to artificial intelligence that seamlessly integrates neural networks with symbolic reasoning systems through a carefully designed modular architecture, achieving state-of-the-art performance while maintaining explainability crucial for trustworthy AI systems.
    </p>
    <div class="project-features">
        <h4>Architecture Pipeline:</h4>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Neural Perception</strong>: BERT-based entity extraction and semantic understanding</li>
            <li><strong>Symbolic Translation</strong>: Predicate generation from neural outputs</li>
            <li><strong>Forward Chaining</strong>: Logical inference with rule application</li>
            <li><strong>Explainable Output</strong>: Complete reasoning traces with confidence scoring</li>
            <li><strong>Confidence Calibration</strong>: Reliable uncertainty quantification for deployment</li>
        </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="project-results">
        <h4>System Integration:</h4>
        <ul>
            <li>Modular design enabling component-level testing and improvement</li>
            <li>Seamless data flow between neural and symbolic components</li>
            <li>Real-time processing capabilities for interactive applications</li>
            <li>Extensible architecture supporting additional reasoning modules</li>
            <li>Comprehensive logging for research reproducibility</li>
        </ul>
    </div>
