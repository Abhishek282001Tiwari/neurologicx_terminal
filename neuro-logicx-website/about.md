## About the Research

### Project Overview

NeuroLogicX represents a significant advancement in artificial intelligence research, bridging the long-standing gap between neural network performance and symbolic system explainability.

### Research Motivation

Current AI systems face a fundamental trade-off:
- **Neural networks** achieve high performance but operate as "black boxes"
- **Symbolic systems** provide explainability but struggle with complex reasoning

NeuroLogicX addresses this challenge through innovative neural-symbolic integration.
